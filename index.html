The Problem
"Many African environments rely on manual processes and disconnected systems — then compound that with unstable connectivity. Generic tech from outside the continent doesn't solve this."

Rigor was founded on a straightforward observation: the infrastructure challenges in African operating environments are real and specific. Unreliable power grids, patchy cellular coverage, heat, dust — these aren't edge cases here, they're the default.

We build embedded systems and IoT solutions that function in these conditions by design. Not as a workaround. Our offline communication architecture keeps devices talking when the internet disappears. Our encrypted drone systems protect critical aerial data. Our automation tools don't assume a stable cloud connection.

This isn't technology for technology's sake. It's engineering shaped by context.
